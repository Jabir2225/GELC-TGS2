Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for the National English Carnival?

Visit our Registration Page and fill out the form. Registration is completely free for all segments!

Can students from outside Dhaka participate?

Absolutely! GELC welcomes students from all districts of Bangladesh. We've had participants from 15+ districts in past events.

How can my school become a partner?

We'd love to partner with your school! Send us an email at gregorianclub@gmail.com with "Partnership Inquiry" in the subject line, and our team will reach out.

Are there any participation fees?

No! All GELC events and segments are completely free. Our mission is to make quality English language platforms accessible to every student.

How can I become a Campus Ambassador?

Fill out the ambassador application on our Registration Page or contact us directly. We look for passionate students who can represent GELC at their institutions.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out using the contact form above or any of our contact methods!
