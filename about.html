Who We Are

The Gregorian English Language Club (GELC) is an official club of St. Gregory's High School & College, dedicated to nurturing English proficiency and creative thinking among students. From literary challenges to pop culture quizzes and large-scale national carnivals, GELC offers platforms for students to express, explore, and excel.

Founded by passionate students and supported by dedicated faculty, we have grown into one of Bangladesh's most recognized student-led English language communities, bringing together thousands of participants from over 100 institutions nationwide.
