Who We Are

Our Story

Founded at St. Gregory's High School & College, Dhaka, the Gregorian English Language Club is one of Bangladesh's most dynamic student-led communities that blends English mastery with creativity, competition, and collaboration.

Our Mission

We nurture English proficiency and creative thinking among students through literary challenges, pop culture quizzes, and large-scale national carnivals that inspire expression and excellence.
