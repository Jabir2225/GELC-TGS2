2018-2019

Foundation Years

The inaugural National English Carnivals (1st & 2nd editions) laid the groundwork for GELC's future success. These pioneering events established our core values of inclusivity, creativity, and excellence. Started with 300+ participants and grew exponentially year after year.

Key Achievements: Established GELC as a recognized name in Dhaka's educational community, created partnerships with multiple schools, and set the template for future carnivals.
