Message from Moderator

A special message from our club moderator.

Ram Kumar Bhattacharya

Moderator, Gregorian English Language Club

On behalf of the Gregorian English Language Club (GELC), I extend my heartfelt gratitude to everyone who made the launch of our first official website possible. This milestone marks a new chapter in our 30-year journey of nurturing linguistic passion and fostering a vibrant community of learners and enthusiasts. Our sincere thanks go to The Gregorian Society (TGS) for their generous support and partnership in powering this platform. We also express deep appreciation to our dedicated members, advisors, and alumni whose relentless efforts and commitment continue to uphold the legacy of GELC. May this website serve as a bridge connecting our past achievements with future aspirations, inspiring many to explore the beauty and power of the English language.
